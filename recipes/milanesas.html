Milanesas

Meat milanesas with potato chips

This is a tipical argentine dish. Milanesas can be made of meat, chicken, eggplants among others.

Today we're going to make the traditional meat milanesas.

Ingredients:

  • Beef top round
  • Grinded hard bread
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Eggs
  • Sunflower oil
  • Potatoes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the meat in thin slices of about 1/16".
  2. Cut the garlic and the parsley into tiny dips.
  3. Break 2 eggs on a bowl, add salt,the garlic and parsley.
  4. Mix and steer the content of the bowl.
  5. Add the slices of meat onto the bowl.
  6. Mix it all making sure that te beef is coveres with the eggs.
  7. Let it sit for about fifteen minutes.

You should put the bread on a plate and proceed to coveres each slice completely on both sided with the bread.

Heat the oil and fry every slice for about 2 minutes each side

Serve with the fried potatoes
