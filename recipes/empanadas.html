Baked beef empanadas

These empanadas are a typical argentine dish

Ingredients

  • 500g grinded beef
  • 250g diced onions
  • 1/4 of red pepper
  • 3 hard boiled eggs
  • "Tapas para empanadas"

Steps

  1. fry the onions, add the pepper diced
  2. add spices
  3. add the beef
  4. cook everything for 15 minutes on low
  5. let it cool down
  6. put the mix on the "tapas para empanadas
  7. make the repulgue
  8. bake at 250Â° C
