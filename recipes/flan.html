Flan with caramel

The flan is awesome and super tasty if made right

The actual, real and awesome flan is plain simple. Shouldnt have any wierd things on it.

The only accepted add-on is whiped cream, thigs like dulce de leche make it unbearably sweet and you can en up with grade 3 diabetes.

Ingredients

  • 500cc of milk
  • 130gr of sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vainilla escence
  • sugar for the caramel of the mold

Steps

  1. heat up the milk with half of the sugar up until it boils up
  2. mix up the eggs with the rest of the sugar
  3. slowly add the hot milk to the eggs and sugar do not stop steering
  4. add the vainilla
  5. pour on a flanera with the previous melted caramel on a pre-heated oven in a bain-marie for approx 45 min
  6. take out, let cool down & un mold it
  7. eat it like there's no tomorrow or until you get grade 1 diabetes.
